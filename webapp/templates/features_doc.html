1. Overview

The Leadway Health Analytics Platform is an internal decision-support tool for the underwriting, pricing, and provider management teams. It automates utilization reporting, benefit benchmarking, corporate plan pricing, and provider analytics — replacing manual Excel-based workflows with instant, branded HTML reports.

Data Handling

  • Date parsing: Handles dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM:SS, Excel serial numbers, and corrupted #### values
  • Effective spend: Uses Amt Paid for paid claims, falls back to Amt Claimed for pipeline (excludes abandoned)
  • Service type normalisation: MTN PHA → Outpatient
  • Disease classification: 16 keyword-based categories covering 100+ diagnosis patterns
  • IBNR estimation: Chain-ladder methodology based on treatment-to-received date lag

2.2 Pricing Tool Live

Split-panel corporate underwriting tool for instant plan quoting with actuarial pricing logic.

📄

Company Risk Intake

Company name, industry (11 sectors), location, total principals + dependants. Industry risk loadings: Oil & Gas +20%, Manufacturing +15%, Banking +5%.

💰

Plan Selection

6 plan cards with real 2026 premiums: PLUS (₦121K), PRO (₦164K), MAX (₦262K), PROMAX (₦383K), MAGNUM (₦734K), MAGNUM+ (₦1.74M).

⚙

Benefit Customisation

Toggle + slider UI for 8 benefits: Dental, Optical, Surgery, ICU Days, NICU, Major Disease, Gym, Immunization. Real-time cost impact.

📈

Pricing Engine

Base premium + upgrade costs (utilisation × avg cost × factor) + industry loading + small group loading (<50 lives) + multi-upgrade loading - volume discount (100+ lives).

🚨

Risk Assessment

Auto risk badge: Low (green) / Moderate (orange) / High (red). High risk = NICU added or Surgery + ICU on premium tier.

📝

Underwriting Decision

Auto-generated: APPROVED / REVIEW REQUIRED / ESCALATE. With detailed underwriter notes citing specific risk factors.

4. Platform Features

🔒

Authentication & Access Control

Login system with email/password. Admin and User roles. Admins can add/remove users and manage access.

📄

Report Management

All generated reports saved with timestamps. Previous reports listed with view and download buttons. HTML reports with embedded styling.

🎨

Leadway Branding

Brand colours from style guide: #C61531 (crimson), #1B1464 (navy), #F15A24 (orange), #262626 (dark). Leadway Health logo on all reports.

📥

Download & Export

Download any report as standalone HTML. Download buttons on individual reports and the previous reports list.

🔧

Auto Column Detection

Smart column mapper handles Leadway export formats: Treatment Date, MemberShipNo, Amt Paid, SERVICE Type (with newlines), and 30+ column aliases.

📅

Robust Date Handling

Parses dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM:SS, Excel serial numbers (30000-60000), and corrupted #### values. Auto-detects format.
